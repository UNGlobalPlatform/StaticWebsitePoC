Workflow

The PoC is implemented as a BPMN, CMMN and DMN complient service

 To deliver trusted data, methods, services and training, the platform needs to be supported by a number of key core services and iin this case the co-ordination of automatic processes and human intervention are orchestrated by this sevice.

Core Service Value

For any processes which have a human component the industry standard BPMN tools provide a great deal of ot of the box functionality. When coupled with DMN business rules and CMMN Case management capabilities most requirments can be satisfied easily.

Implementation

The PoC platform is running the open source Camunda BPM suite.
