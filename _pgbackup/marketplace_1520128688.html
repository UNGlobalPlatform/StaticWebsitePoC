Marketplace

Whilst there is a great deal of interesting data and peer reviewed work available it is very diffecult to find it, the marketplace is intended to allow data collaboratives to be created and find data, methods, and partners easily to solve the statistical challenges

 To deliver trusted data, methods, services and training, the platform needs to be supported by a number of key core services and the marketplace is a key enabler for the success of the platform.

Core Service Value

The marketplace allows interested parties to find each other, the projects and outcomes they are interested in and the increase quality whilst decreasing cost.

The example is a Magento based site with some customisation of the site templates.
